## Leadership & Teamwork

I genuinely enjoy working in teams — it exposes me to broader perspectives that I wouldn't get to on my own, and it often pushes me to think in ways I was unconsciously limiting myself to when working solo. I believe good teamwork, combined with clear communication and social interaction, directly increases efficiency.

A few things that matter to me in how I work with others:

- **Inclusiveness** — I make sure everyone around me gets a fair chance to be involved in the conversation and doesn't feel left out.
- **Respect** — I respect others' opinions and perspectives, even when they differ from mine.
- **Quick learning** — I pick up new concepts quickly, which helps me contribute fast in unfamiliar problem spaces during hackathons and team projects.
